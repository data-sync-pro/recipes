Data Sync Pro (DSP) unifies five purpose-built engines that span data processing, automation, and user interaction — all sharing the same record-based, declarative framework.

  • #Batch — Handles large-volume processing. Replaces Apex batch jobs with declarative transformations, data enrich, cleansing, and high-performance org-to-org migrations.
  • #Trigger — Executes bulkified automation on DML events (insert, update, delete, undelete), bringing rules-driven simplicity to transformations, validations, and downstream actions.
  • #Data List — Turns queries into interactive, configurable tables with rules-driven actions, letting business users filter, explore, edit, and act on records at the list or row level.
  • #Action Button — Adds configurable buttons to the Lightning Pages or Data Lists for guided actions, with support for pre-populated transformations, line editors and workflows.
  • #Data Loader — Enables reusable CSV imports with built-in transformation logic, delivering secure, auditable, and scalable data loading across Salesforce orgs.

Together, these engines form DSP's unified rules-driven foundation: modular, flexible, and enterprise-grade — with declarative simplicity at its core.

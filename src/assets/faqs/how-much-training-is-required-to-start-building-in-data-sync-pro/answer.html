Very little. DSP is built natively on Salesforce (Apex and LWC) and introduces just seven intuitive configuration objects — Connection, Direction, Pipeline, Executable, Field Mapping, Variable, and Schedule. Most are self-explanatory by name.

Building processes is as simple as creating records on these objects, with a one-time setup for Apex Triggers or Lightning Pages. Each rules engine comes with a guided interface that walks you through configuration step by step, with smart features like autocomplete for filters, mappings, and formulas.

User manuals and starter recipes are included, and most teams are building useful rules within just a few hours.
